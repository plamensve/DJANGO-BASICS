Caprese Salad

Cuisine Type: Italian

Cooking Time: 10min.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions: Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into even slices. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating with basil leaves. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the top. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
